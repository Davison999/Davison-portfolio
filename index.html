01 — About Me

The Man Behind The Data

My name is Davison Swai, a third-year Bachelor of Data Science student at the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC) in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. My passion for data was ignited when I realised that the answers to the most pressing questions in business, health, and society are already hidden in plain sight buried in data, waiting to be found.

What sets me apart is my unique combination of machine learning engineering and business intelligence expertise. While most analysts stop at generating numbers, I go further — translating statistical outputs into interactive Power BI dashboards that executives can act on in real time.

"Without data, you're just another person with an opinion. My job is to make sure the data speaks — loudly, clearly, and at the right time."
